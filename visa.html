Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What does the $50 consultation fee cover? +

The $50 fee covers a one-on-one consultation session with a visa expert. This includes document review, application strategy, country-specific requirements, and a full Q&A session. Visa application fees and admin fees are separate and quoted after the consultation.

When will I get my call? +

We aim to schedule your consultation call within 48 hours of receiving your completed form and payment confirmation. During high-volume periods, this may take slightly longer, but we always prioritise timely responses.

Are visa fees included in the $50? +

No. The $50 is strictly the consultation fee for our expert guidance. Government visa application fees, admin fees, and any other charges are entirely separate and will be clearly communicated to you during your consultation.

What countries can you help with? +

We provide consultation for visa applications to virtually any country worldwide — including the UK, Schengen Area, USA, Canada, UAE, Australia, and more. Our student visa service specifically covers Europe, UK, Canada and the USA.

Can you help if I've been refused before? +

Absolutely. We specialise in helping applicants who have previously been refused. During your consultation, we'll analyse the reason for refusal and develop a stronger strategy for your reapplication.

How do I know my payment is safe? +

Only send payment to our verified Mobile Money number (+233 24 441 6725) or after receiving bank details from our official email: info@theroyalaccentcreations.com. We are not responsible for transactions made to any other accounts.
